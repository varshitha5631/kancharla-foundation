Visionary Leader and Changemaker

Dr. Sreekanth C
Kancharla

Dr. Sreekanth C Kancharla, BTech, MBA, MIE, is the dynamic founder of
Kancharla Foundation. His mission to eradicate poverty and uplift rural
communities has inspired impactful programs in education, health, and
sustainable development.

Key Achievements:

Upskilled over 4,000 youth by establishing Airtel BPO in rural areas.

Fostered organic farming practices and empowered women through skill development.

Recognitions:

Honored with the Best Rural Entrepreneur Award by Vice-President of India, Sri M. Venkaiah Naidu.

Twice awarded the Educational Leadership Award at IIT-Delhi. Received the NTR Seva Award for flood relief efforts in 2009.

Awarded the Mother Teresa Sadhbhavana Award by the International Business Council.v

Recognized as 'Our Neighbourhood Heroes' by HDFC Bank for services during the COVID-19 pandemic.

Community Service:

Organized blood donation camps and fundraisers.

Contributed food and essential aid to disaster victims.

Planted thousands of saplings to promote environmental conservation.
